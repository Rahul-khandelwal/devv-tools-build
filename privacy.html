1. 100% Client-Side Processing

At DevvTools, we take your privacy extremely seriously. All developer tools on this website operate entirely within your browser. We do not have backend servers that receive, process, or store the code, tokens, or strings you input into our tools.

Whether you are formatting sensitive JSON data, decoding JWTs, or testing Regex patterns, your data remains secure and never leaves your device.

2. Local Storage

To provide a seamless experience, we use your browser's local storage to save your input state across sessions (so if you accidentally close a tab, your work isn't lost). This data is compressed and securely stored only on your local hard drive. We have no access to it.

3. Google Analytics & AdSense

To keep DevvTools free, we use Google AdSense to display advertisements and Google Analytics to understand basic site traffic (like page views). These third-party services may use cookies and similar technologies to collect non-personal, aggregated data about your visit.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
